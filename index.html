GTA V settings.xml Parser

Tired of taking screenshots of your settings to share in discussions online?

Paste the contents of your GTA 5 settings file (found at /Users/Username/Documents/Rockstar Games/GTA V/settings.xml) in the left area,
hit the button, and a nicely formatted settings block will appear on the right, ready to copy paste into your favorite comment/discussion.

Work in progress. Check this Github repository or this reddit thread for progress.
